⚠️ **Criteria for Installment Payments:**

  1. **Initial Deposit:** A minimum down payment (e.g., 10%) is often required (not calculated here).
  2. **Documentation:** Valid ID and proof of address.
  3. **Consistency:** Monthly payments must be made on time to avoid contract termination.
  4. **Possession:** Land possession and building rights are typically granted after 50-70% of the payment is completed.
