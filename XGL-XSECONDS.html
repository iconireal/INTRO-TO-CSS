Our Services

  • Interstate Delivery

    What it is: Delivery of packages between states in South-South and South-West Nigeria.
    Why choose this: Best for long-distance shipments that require secure, documented handover across state borders.

  • Local Delivery

    What it is: Delivery within the same city or town.
    Why choose this: Fastest delivery option for urgent, same-day dispatches or small-radius errands.

    • Vehicle Options

    • Cars

      Use for: Medium-sized deliveries that need extra protection (e.g. electronics, clothes).
      Why choose this: Balanced speed, safety, and weather protection.

    • Bikes

      Use for: Small items and urgent deliveries (e.g. documents, parcels).
      Why choose this: Fastest option for local delivery, can navigate traffic easily.

    • Trunk

      Use for: Bulk deliveries, furniture, large goods.
      Why choose this: Large capacity and suited for moving items across towns or states.
